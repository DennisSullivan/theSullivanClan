The Sullivan Clan

A Family Chronicle in the Spirit of Americana

Welcome

This is the story of an American family — its journeys, its joys, its quiet triumphs, and the threads of love and duty that bind one generation to the next. Inspired by the warm, human storytelling of Norman Rockwell, this archive preserves the moments that shaped the Sullivan legacy.

Our Roots

From small‑town beginnings to service in World War II, from wedding vows in 1946 to the growing branches of a family tree, the Sullivan story is woven into the fabric of 20th‑century America. These pages honor the lives of Bill and Lois Sullivan and the generations who followed.
